FAQs

Cornerstone Church holds services every Sunday at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM, with a midweek service on Wednesdays at 7:00 PM. Special holiday services and events are announced on the church’s website and social media.

You can get involved by joining a small group, volunteering in various ministries, or attending newcomer events. Visit our welcome center after a service or check our website for upcoming opportunities.

You can give online through our secure giving portal on the church website using a debit/credit card or bank transfer. Recurring donations and one-time gifts can be set up easily through your online account.
