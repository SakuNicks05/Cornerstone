Stories of Faith & Transformation

At Cornerstone, we believe in the power of personal testimonies. These stories reflect how God is working in the lives of our church family. Be encouraged as you read how faith has changed lives!

"I was at my lowest point, struggling with anxiety and uncertainty about my future. When I joined Cornerstone, I was welcomed with open arms, and through prayer and support, I found peace in God's plan. Now, I face each day with faith, knowing that He is in control."
- Elizabeth Jordan

"I used to believe I had to carry my burdens alone, but through this church, I discovered the true meaning of God’s grace. The sermons and community helped me let go of guilt and embrace the love of Christ. My life has been completely transformed, and I now walk in freedom and joy! "
- Elizabeth Jordan

"After moving to a new city, I felt lost and disconnected—until I found Cornerstone. The friendships I’ve made here have strengthened my faith and reminded me that I’m never alone. I am so grateful for a church that truly feels like family!"
- Elizabeth Jordan
